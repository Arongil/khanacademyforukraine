Empowering Ukrainian Students Through Education

For 856 days, Ukraine has been at war, leaving 2 in 5 kids unable to attend school. But what if we could change this story? What if instead of falling behind, kids could get ahead?

Meet Nazar Drugov, a Ukrainian high school student who defied the odds. Using free Khan Academy lessons during blackouts and in shelters during bombardments, Nazar secured admission to Tufts University.

However, there's a challenge: Khan Academy is primarily in English, and only 1 in 100 Ukrainians know English.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to translate and localize Khan Academy content into Ukrainian, making STEM education accessible to all 4 million students in Ukraine.

  • We've already translated algebra basics into Ukrainian.
  • Our next goal is to translate arithmetic and precalculus.
  • We're collaborating with schools to reach every student in Ukraine.

Join us in our effort to provide every Ukrainian student with a free, world-class education through Khan Academy.
